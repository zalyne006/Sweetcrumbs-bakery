About Us.

Founded in 2010 by Grace & Samuel Kariuki with a single oven

and a dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Over the years,we have grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousands of families, offices and event planners accross the city.Our bakers rise before dawn every single day ro ensure that what lands on your table is always fresh,always flavorful, and always made with care.

We believe baking is an art,not a factory process.That is why we never use artificial preservatives, and we source our flour,eggs and dairy from certified local farms within 100km of Nairobi.
